About Us

Whos is Revive?

At Revive Church, we are passionate about seeing lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ. Located in heart of Grand Rapids , Michigan our church is vibrant, welcoming community where people from all walks of life come together to worship, grow, and serve.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and equip individuals to live out their faith in practical, life-changing ways. We aim to revive hearts, renew minds, and restore hope through the teaching of God's word, meaningful worship, and community outreach.

What We Believe

Scripture: We believe in the authority of the Bible as God's inspired and living Word, providing guidance and truth for our lives.

Jesus Christ: We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, whose life, death, and resurrection offer salvation to all who believe.

Community: We believe in the importance of the church as a community of believers who support, encourage, and challenge one another in their faith journey.

Transformation: We believe that a relationship with Jesus brings personal and communal transformation, leading to a life of purpose, joy, and service.
